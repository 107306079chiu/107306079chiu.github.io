Door Understands You

1 Introduction

Automatic doors in public places often open unnecessary when somebody just passes by. Methods such as touch pad / lower the sensitivity were implemented to solve this issue. However, hand hygeine problem / delayed problem emerged.

2 Solution

An intelligent automatic door system is proposed. A low-resolution thermal sensor is used to collect real-time thermal image, a U-Net-based model is used for segmentation, and CNN is implemented to perform prediction. When people raise hand, the door will open.

3 Result

With the system, automatic door will not open when no one intends to get in. The door will open by a clean (touchless), energy saving (open accurately), privacy (thermal & low-resolution) way. -- special thanks: W. Su, K. Wang, T. Hsu -- 2021 IEEE ECICE (pending), co-author: S. Chiu, Y. Tu, C. Hsu

Manage the Package

1 Introduction

With numerous packages to be sent and so many third party logistics (3PL) providers to choose, this project implement a optimization method to find the most suitable combination between packages and 3PL. The combination matches requirement of package to 3PL term, acheiving objectives including lowest cost & shortest delivery days to send pakages.

2 Solution

In the proposed system, Non-dominated Sorting Genetic Algorithm (NSGA-II) and flexible deliver model is implemented. The list of packages and 3PL service terms will be encoded into gene. After iterations and flexible deliver model, the output is a list which assigned packages to different 3PL in order to acheive the two objectives mentioned above.

3 Result

In comparison to null model (random assignment) & MOPSO assigning packages, the system improved performance of total cost under same delivery days by 46%. -- published in 2021 ICIM, co-author: Y. Tu, C. Chiu
