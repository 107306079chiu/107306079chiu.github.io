Door Understands You

1 Introduction

Automatic doors in public places often open unnecessary when somebody just passes by. Methods such as touch pad / lower the sensitivity were implemented to solve this issue. However, hand hygeine problem / delayed problem emerged.

2 Solution

An intelligent automatic door system is proposed. A low-resolution thermal sensor is used to collect real-time thermal image, a U-Net-based model is used for segmentation, and CNN is implemented to perform prediction. When people raise hand, the door will open.

3 Result

With the system, automatic door will not open when no one intends to get in. The door will open by a clean (touchless), energy saving (open accurately), privacy (thermal & low-resolution) way. -- special thanks: W. Su, K. Wang, T. Hsu, and MOST College Research Scolarship -- 2021 IEEE ECICE (pending), co-author: S. Chiu, Y. Tu, C. Hsu

Manage the Package

1 Introduction

With numerous packages to be sent and so many third party logistics (3PL) providers to choose, this project implement a optimization method to find the most suitable combination between packages and 3PL. The combination matches requirement of package to 3PL term, acheiving objectives including lowest cost & shortest delivery days to send pakages.

2 Solution

In the proposed system, Non-dominated Sorting Genetic Algorithm (NSGA-II) and flexible deliver model is implemented. The list of packages and 3PL service terms will be encoded into gene. After iterations and flexible deliver model, the output is a list which assigned packages to different 3PL in order to acheive the two objectives mentioned above.

3 Result

In comparison to null model (random assignment) & MOPSO assigning packages, the system improved performance of total cost under same delivery days by 46%. -- special thanks: MOST College Research Scolarship -- published in 2021 ICIM, co-author: Y. Tu, C. Chiu

Morning Alarm - Less is More

1 Introduction

Depth of sleep is different by stages in sleep cycle. People in deep sleep might need a louder alarm to get awake; shallow sleep stage only needs light-music alarm. Less is more.

2 Solution

In this project, we classify music on spotify by features including bpm, speechiness, energy, and so on to determine when an alarm rings, which song should be played. FCN is used to perform classification. (Another important part is how to know a person's sleep cycle stage... maybe from a Fitbit? However, in this experiment, we assume we know it.)

3 Result

Training data is collected by Spotify api for total 15,000 data, x = bpm, speechiness, energy...; y = relax/party/workout music... (manual labled from songs in playlists). val_accuracy = 0.9842 and cross entropy was used as loss function. -- group members: R. Yang, I. Lin, Y. Wang -- course info: 2020SS Computer Programming

You Safe, I happy

1 Introduction

Traffic accidents cause losses in life and property and bring about chain reaction accidents or traffic jams. Hence, an effective method to understand historical data and forecast the accident risk is of great significance.

2 Solution

We include both environmental data and traffic conditions as the data and conduct several ML model to predict accident risk on the freeway. The experiment result shows that SVM performed better than other method and has reached 75% of accuracy. It is expected to play the role of decision support for the public sector in improving the transportation system.

3 Group Info.

-- group members: S. Lin, T. Cheng, C. Zhu -- intern project @ CECI, instructors: Y. Lee, F. Su, Q. Hsieh

Muscle Flow

1 Introduction

畢業專題: 健身 Android app

2 Solution

這是一款為健身初學者和學生建造的APP，主打「配對諮詢」、「Tutor 專屬諮詢」，來同時達到降低費用及提供專業、客製化教學的目的。另外，App 內亦設置了「健力三項視覺分析系統」來幫助使用者維持訓練動作的正確性，與「虛擬角色成長遊戲」，希望能為枯燥的訓練過程增添新鮮感。透過上述服務，本應用程式不但能降低健身的進入門檻、提升運動意願，也能同時為市場上的自由教練提供新的客戶來源。
